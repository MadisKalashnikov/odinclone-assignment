Positions

There is so much more to volleyball than just rotating around the court. There are seven different positions in volleyball, and each player has a specific role to play. In order to take your understanding of the sport of volleyball to the next level, it is important to know the significance of each position. outside hitter (aka OH, outside, pin, left side), opposite hitter (aka OPP, pin, right side), middle Blocker (aka MB, middle hitter, middle), setter (aka S), libero (aka L), defensive specialists (aka DS), serving specialist (aka SS).
